Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

This site will have pictures of video game consoles, guides to fix common issues, and price info for games and systems.

Where will you get all of the content?

Images will come from official sources and research, and the text will be based on info from collector sites and personal knowledge.

Why should users care about the site?

If you collect games or just want to know how much your old console is worth, this site will have everything in one place.

Why make this site?

There aren’t many sites that organize this kind of info without making you dig through forums. This one will be simple and easy to use.

What will users do on the site?

They can check out console images, look up troubleshooting tips, compare consoles, and see game prices.

What’s the goal of the site?

To be a super easy-to-use reference for video game collectors and gamers who want quick, accurate info.
