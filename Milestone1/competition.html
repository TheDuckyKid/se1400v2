Competition

What are other sites doing right?

Sites like PriceCharting and GameFAQs are pretty useful. PriceCharting keeps track of game prices, and GameFAQs has a ton of guides made by other gamers. There are also forums and YouTube channels where collectors share tips and info.

What are they doing wrong?

A lot of these sites can be messy or hard to use. Forums are full of old posts, and it’s tough to tell what info is actually legit. Price guides don’t always show trends over time, and troubleshooting help is all over the place, so you have to dig through a bunch of sites to find what you need.

How will this site be better?

This site keeps things simple and easy to find. No more scrolling through endless forum threads or sketchy websites—everything will be in one place. Clear guides, updated prices, and troubleshooting tips without all the extra hassle.
